## Description / Scope Summary

- **Service Required:** Contractor to provide boxed noon meals (sub sandwiches) for Armed Forces applicants at MEPS.
- **Days of Service:** Monday–Friday (excludes legal holidays/training days), plus up to 28 Saturdays/year (approx. 264 days annually), including some extended/mission hours.
- **Meal Preparation:** Must be in a licensed facility/restaurant; contractor handles preparation, delivery, receipt collection, and all logistics except dining area/waste facilities (provided by MEPS).
- **Security & Training:** All contractor staff must complete AT Level I, IWATCH, and OPSEC training.
- **Subcontracting Allowed:** Per FAR guidelines; must notify if supplying food from a source other than identified in the proposal with requisite health certificates.
- **Special Requirements:** Contractor responsible for own transportation/screening/parking; must maintain food safety standards; government only provides tables/chairs/waste cans.
